## Key Findings

### December 2025: Deadliest Month
- **7 deaths total** in ICE custody (Austin Kocher verified)
- **5 of those deaths** are now in our verified database
- **Context:** Record detention population of 68,442 coincided with deaths
- **Congressional Response:** Multiple Congress members demanded investigations
- **Medical Care Concerns:** Reports of inadequate care at multiple facilities

### Source Quality Issues Identified
1. **ICE.gov website redirecting:** Official death reporting page not accessible (redirects to DHS NTAS)
2. **Delayed reporting:** ICE required to report deaths within 30 days, but website hasn't been updated since October 2025
3. **Transparency gaps:** Makes independent verification difficult

### Austin Kocher's Tracking Work
- **Most reliable source** for ICE detention deaths
- Provides names, dates, facilities, causes
- Includes context about facility conditions
- Cites original reporting (NOTUS, El Paso Times, Reuters)
- Cross-references Congressional statements
